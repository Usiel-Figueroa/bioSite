Violeta Figueroa

A Journey of Dedication and Empowerment

Violeta is a devoted sister and a proud mother of four, roles that bring her immense joy and fulfillment. Beyond her family life, she is a passionate entrepreneur who has turned her talent and dedication into a thriving career. After earning her cosmetology license and mastering skills like haircuts, perms, and other beauty services, she took a bold step forward. In the past year, Violeta achieved a significant milestone by opening her own salon, a space where her vision for beauty and community comes to life.
