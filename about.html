About Violeta Figueroa

A Journey of Dedication and Empowerment

Violeta Figueroa is a proud mother of four and a passionate entrepreneur who turned her dreams into reality. With a license in cosmetology, she has honed her skills in haircuts, perms, and a range of beauty services. In 2024, Violeta took a bold step and opened her own business, Lions Hair Salon, a vibrant space dedicated to enhancing beauty and confidence in her community.

Her hard work and dedication were celebrated during the salon’s ribbon-cutting ceremony, where the city mayor honored her for her contributions to the local economy and community well-being. Lions Hair Salon is not just a business—it is a reflection of Violeta’s vision to create a welcoming space for clients to feel empowered and rejuvenated.

Beyond her professional achievements, Violeta cherishes her role as a mother, finding joy in supporting her children’s growth and accomplishments. In her free time, she enjoys dancing, traveling, and sharing quality time with her big family, which bring balance and inspiration to her busy life.

Violeta’s journey exemplifies resilience, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to her goals. Through Lions Hair Salon, she continues to inspire others to pursue their dreams and celebrate their individuality.
